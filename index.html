About Me

I obtained my bachelor's degree from Tianjin University, and I am currently pursuing a Master of Engineering (MEng) degree at the same institution. My research interests primarily revolve around cementitious materials, with a specific focus on enhancing their durability and exploring potential modifications to improve their properties. I am particularly enthusiastic about researching low-carbon and carbon-neutral concrete, as I believe these areas are critical for sustainable construction and environmental preservation.

As I continue my academic journey, I am actively seeking opportunities to pursue a Ph.D. program in a field that aligns with my research interests. I am passionate about making meaningful contributions to the development of environmentally friendly construction materials and am eager to explore doctoral programs that will allow me to delve deeper into these areas and contribute to the global efforts for a more sustainable and resilient built environment.
